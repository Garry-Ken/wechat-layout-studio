# Product Packaging Notes

## MVP Product Lines

1. Article acquisition MVP: material inbox, topic input, knowledge retrieval, article generation, scoring, WeChat layout, rich-text copy, and image-card export.
2. Video distribution MVP: Douyin/Video Account short script, YouTube outline, X/Twitter thread, WeChat Moments copy, image-card copy, and publishing prediction.

Custom delivery is not a third generic product. It is a paid implementation of the two MVPs against the client's own Feishu/Lark, Obsidian, monitoring sources, approval flow, and channel requirements.

## Pricing Logic

Do not sell only a webpage for high-ticket custom work. Sell a working content acquisition system tied to the client's private knowledge and channels.

- Self-serve: low-price one-time license or subscription.
- Pro: higher monthly or annual fee for multi-channel output and monitoring.
- Custom baseline: CNY 30k is reasonable for one private knowledge source, one content channel, and a controlled approval flow.
- Custom advanced: CNY 50k-80k is reasonable when it includes Feishu/Lark plus Obsidian, vertical monitoring, WeChat draft creation, video scripts, image cards, training, and a 2-4 week delivery window.
- Maintenance: charge separately for monitoring source updates, connector changes, prompt/rubric calibration, analytics reports, and new channel adapters.

## Delivery Boundaries

Define these before quoting:

- Number of private knowledge sources.
- Whether connectors use official API, browser automation, manual upload, or local files.
- Number of monitored external sources and update frequency.
- Output channels and formats.
- Whether the system creates drafts only or can publish after confirmation.
- Data privacy, authorization, and credential ownership.
- Acceptance criteria: example topics, expected article quality, script quality, scoring report, and approval flow.
